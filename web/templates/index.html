About this Website

This website was created for CutieHack to help students easily discover the organizations that match their interests. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can browse through UCR's student organizations and quickly find communities they want to join. Whether you're looking for professional development, cultural groups, or social clubs, this platform streamlines the search process. Our goal is to make getting involved on campus easier, faster, and more enjoyable for every student.
